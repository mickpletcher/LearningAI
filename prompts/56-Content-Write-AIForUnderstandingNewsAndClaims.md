# Prompt 56

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Create a practical page showing how beginners can use AI to better understand news stories, public claims, and confusing information without blindly trusting the output.

Goal:
Help regular people use AI to summarize, compare, and question claims more effectively while staying careful about accuracy.

Create this new file:

- src/pages/ai-for-understanding-news-and-claims.astro

Also review and update if needed:

- src/pages/everyday-uses.astro

- src/pages/use-cases.astro

- src/pages/safety-and-privacy.astro

- src/pages/faq.astro

- src/components/PromptBox.astro

Page content goals:
Cover practical uses such as:

- simplifying a confusing article or topic

- comparing two explanations of the same issue

- identifying what questions to ask about a claim

- spotting missing context

- turning a dense topic into plain English

- building a fact checking checklist

- separating opinion from verifiable claims

Important content requirements:

- explain clearly that AI can help organize and question information, but should not be treated as a final source of truth

- remind users to verify important claims with reliable sources

- explain that AI can hallucinate, miss context, or present false confidence

- include copy and paste prompts for checking understanding and thinking critically

- keep examples neutral and practical rather than political or sensational

Suggested page structure:

- title and intro

- how AI can help you understand a claim

- how AI can help you fact check more thoughtfully

- sample prompts

- what to watch out for

- where to go next

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- calm

- direct

- beginner friendly

- respectful

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not present AI as a fact checker that replaces real sources

- do not encourage political persuasion content

- do not turn the page into a media criticism rant

- do not overload the page with too many prompts

- do not make claims without showing how to verify them

Implementation requirements:

- keep the page visually consistent with the site

- use existing components where helpful

- ensure related links work correctly

- preserve Astro structure and shared patterns

Output requirement:
Generate the full contents of the new page and any supporting updates if needed.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Write the news and claims page.
