# Prompt 64

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Audit the site’s examples, scenarios, and prompt use cases for consistency, variety, and realism.

Goal:
Make sure the site uses practical examples that feel believable, useful, and well distributed across the content.

Files to review:

- all files in src/pages/

- all Markdown files in src/content/lessons/

- shared components that contain example text or prompt examples

Audit focus:

1. are examples realistic for everyday people

2. are too many pages using the exact same examples

3. do life stage pages have examples that fit their audience

4. do examples match the page purpose

5. do prompt examples feel copy and paste ready

6. are examples varied enough across work, school, family, travel, budgeting, and daily life

Tasks:

- replace repetitive examples with stronger and more varied ones

- improve unrealistic or vague scenarios

- make sure examples feel practical and grounded

- align examples better with each page’s audience and purpose

- improve prompts where they feel too generic

- reduce repeated story patterns across the site

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- direct

- respectful

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not remove strong examples just to force variety

- do not invent fake data or fake case studies

- do not turn examples into long stories

- do not generate an audit report without making real fixes

- do not rewrite everything if only parts need improvement

Implementation requirements:

- directly update the relevant files

- preserve Astro structure and working links

- keep the examples useful, short, and easy to understand

- ensure better example variety improves the overall user experience

Output requirement:
Directly update the relevant files to improve example consistency and variety.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Fix the site’s examples.
