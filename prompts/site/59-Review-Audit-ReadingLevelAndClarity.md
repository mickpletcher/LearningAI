# Prompt 59

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Audit the site's reading level and overall clarity so it stays accessible to beginners without sounding dumbed down.

Goal:
Make the site easier to understand for everyday readers while preserving useful substance and practical value.

Files to review:

- all files in src/pages/

- all Markdown files in src/content/lessons/

- shared components that include user facing educational copy

Audit focus:

1. sentence length

2. paragraph length

3. jargon density

4. clarity of explanations

5. whether abstract ideas are grounded in real examples

6. whether the reading level feels approachable for non technical adults

7. whether pages become too repetitive when simplified

Tasks:

- shorten overly long sentences

- break up heavy paragraphs

- replace unclear jargon with plain English

- improve examples where explanations feel abstract

- smooth out awkward phrasing

- tighten bloated sections

- keep the writing approachable and practical

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- calm

- direct

- respectful

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not remove useful meaning just to make text shorter

- do not oversimplify until the content becomes vague

- do not make the tone childish

- do not produce an audit report without making the fixes directly

- do not rewrite everything unnecessarily

Implementation requirements:

- directly update the relevant files

- preserve Astro structure and working links

- keep page purpose intact while improving clarity

- make sure simplified text still sounds natural

Output requirement:
Directly update the relevant files to improve reading level and clarity.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Fix the site’s clarity and reading level.
