# Prompt 51

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Create a practical page showing how beginners can use AI to help with health related admin tasks and paperwork without using AI as a doctor or a source of diagnosis.

Goal:
Help regular people use AI for organizing, understanding, and drafting around healthcare logistics while keeping the page safe, calm, and practical.

Create this new file:

- src/pages/ai-for-health-admin-and-paperwork.astro

Also review and update if needed:

- src/pages/everyday-uses.astro

- src/pages/use-cases.astro

- src/pages/safety-and-privacy.astro

- src/pages/safe-prompting.astro

- src/components/PromptBox.astro

Page content goals:
Cover practical uses such as:

- simplifying confusing paperwork or instructions

- drafting questions for a doctor visit

- organizing appointment notes

- building a medication or appointment checklist

- summarizing general medical information into simpler language

- preparing a list of symptoms or questions to discuss with a professional

- organizing insurance or billing questions in plain English

Important content requirements:

- clearly explain that AI is not a doctor and should not replace medical advice, diagnosis, or emergency care

- remind users not to paste detailed medical records, insurance IDs, policy numbers, prescription numbers, Social Security numbers, or other sensitive health information

- include safe copy and paste prompts focused on general understanding and organization

- explain when to verify information with a healthcare professional

- keep the page practical and non alarmist

Suggested page structure:

- title and intro

- how AI can help with health admin tasks

- safe ways to use AI around paperwork and planning

- sample prompts

- what to watch out for

- where to go next

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- calm

- respectful

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

- not fear based

Important constraints:

- do not provide diagnosis guidance

- do not provide treatment recommendations

- do not encourage users to rely on AI in emergencies

- do not suggest sharing sensitive medical data

- do not make the page sound like a telehealth ad

- do not overload the page with too many prompts

Implementation requirements:

- keep the page visually consistent with the site

- use existing components where helpful

- ensure related links work correctly

- preserve Astro structure and shared patterns

Output requirement:
Generate the full contents of the new page and any supporting updates if needed.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Write the health admin and paperwork page.
