# Prompt 52

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Create a practical page showing how beginners can use AI to compare products, organize shopping decisions, and think through purchases more clearly.

Goal:
Help regular people use AI as a shopping comparison helper without treating it like a final authority on prices, specs, or deals.

Create this new file:

- src/pages/ai-for-shopping-and-product-comparison.astro

Also review and update if needed:

- src/pages/ai-for-everyday-budgeting.astro

- src/pages/everyday-uses.astro

- src/pages/use-cases.astro

- src/pages/prompt-templates.astro

- src/components/PromptBox.astro

Page content goals:
Cover practical uses such as:

- comparing product categories

- narrowing down options

- turning a list of features into a pros and cons comparison

- planning questions to ask before buying

- comparing tools, appliances, subscriptions, or travel gear

- matching products to a budget or a use case

- simplifying confusing spec language into plain English

Important content requirements:

- explain that AI can help organize and explain options but should not be trusted for final prices, inventory, warranty terms, or current availability

- remind users to verify specs and seller details from the official source

- include copy and paste prompts for product comparison

- keep examples realistic for normal households and everyday buying decisions

- include safe prompting reminders around personal account or payment data

Suggested page structure:

- title and intro

- how AI can help with shopping decisions

- how AI can help compare products

- sample prompts

- what to watch out for

- where to go next

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- direct

- calm

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not claim AI finds the best deal automatically

- do not provide fake prices or fake comparisons

- do not encourage sharing payment details or account information

- do not turn the page into affiliate style content

- do not overload the page with too many prompts

Implementation requirements:

- keep the page visually consistent with the site

- use existing components where helpful

- ensure related links work correctly

- preserve Astro structure and shared patterns

Output requirement:
Generate the full contents of the new page and any supporting updates if needed.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Write the shopping and product comparison page.
