# Prompt 74

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Audit the site's internal content hierarchy and improve how pages relate to one another.

Goal:
Make the site structure feel logical, layered, and easy for beginners to follow as they move from broad topics to specific help.

Files to review:

- all files in src/pages/

- all Markdown files in src/content/lessons/

- shared navigation and layout files

- lesson index and use case hub pages if they exist

Audit focus:

1. whether broad overview pages link naturally to more specific pages

2. whether specific pages point back to the right overview pages

3. whether the content has a clear hierarchy

4. whether some pages feel orphaned or hard to find

5. whether the beginner journey from homepage to deeper content is clear

6. whether pages are competing instead of supporting one another

Tasks:

- improve linking between broad and specific pages

- strengthen page relationships so the site feels organized by topic and intent

- reduce cases where pages overlap without clear hierarchy

- improve section labels and next steps where content hierarchy feels weak

- make sure deeper pages are surfaced from the right parent pages

- improve discoverability for useful content without cluttering navigation

Examples of hierarchy patterns to support:

- broad page to specific guide

- life stage page to targeted use page

- lesson index to lessons

- use case hub to specific solution pages

- safety and prompting pages cross linked cleanly

- work pages clustered together logically

Tone requirements:

- practical

- clear

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not redesign the entire site architecture without reason

- do not clutter pages with too many links

- do not generate an audit report without making real fixes

- do not create artificial hierarchy where content does not support it

Implementation requirements:

- directly update the relevant files

- preserve Astro structure and working links

- improve the site map feel without overcomplicating navigation

- keep the user journey smooth and understandable

Output requirement:
Directly update the relevant files to improve internal content hierarchy.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Fix the site’s content hierarchy.
