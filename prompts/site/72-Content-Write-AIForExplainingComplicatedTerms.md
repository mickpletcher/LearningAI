# Prompt 72

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Create a practical page showing how beginners can use AI to think through decisions, compare options, and organize pros and cons more clearly.

Goal:
Help regular people use AI as a thinking and organizing tool for everyday decisions without treating it like a final decision maker.

Create this new file:

- src/pages/ai-for-decision-making-and-pros-cons.astro

Also review and update if needed:

- src/pages/everyday-uses.astro

- src/pages/use-cases.astro

- src/pages/ai-for-shopping-and-product-comparison.astro

- src/pages/ai-for-comparing-services-and-subscriptions.astro

- src/pages/prompt-templates.astro

- src/components/PromptBox.astro

Page content goals:
Cover practical uses such as:

- comparing two or three options

- building a pros and cons list

- identifying missing questions

- clarifying priorities before choosing

- comparing tradeoffs such as cost, convenience, time, and risk

- turning messy thoughts into a clearer decision framework

- making personal or household decisions easier to think through

Important content requirements:

- explain that AI can help organize thinking, but should not make important choices for the user

- remind users to verify facts, costs, rules, and terms before deciding

- include copy and paste prompts for comparing options and listing tradeoffs

- keep examples realistic for normal households, work decisions, and daily life

- explain how to avoid overtrusting neat sounding summaries

Suggested page structure:

- title and intro

- how AI can help you think through a decision

- how AI can help compare pros and cons

- sample prompts

- what to watch out for

- where to go next

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- calm

- direct

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not present AI as a life decision maker

- do not imply AI can know the right choice for a person

- do not overload the page with too many prompts

- do not make the page sound like self help coaching

- do not encourage sharing highly sensitive personal details

Implementation requirements:

- keep the page visually consistent with the site

- use existing components where helpful

- ensure internal links work correctly

- preserve Astro structure and shared patterns

Output requirement:
Generate the full contents of the new page and any supporting updates if needed.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Write the decision making and pros and cons page.
