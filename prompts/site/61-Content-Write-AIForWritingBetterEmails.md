# Prompt 61

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Create a practical page showing how beginners can use AI to write better emails for everyday life, work, school, and customer service situations.

Goal:
Help regular people use AI to draft clearer, more polite, and more effective emails without sounding robotic or losing their own voice.

Create this new file:

- src/pages/ai-for-writing-better-emails.astro

Also review and update if needed:

- src/pages/everyday-uses.astro

- src/pages/workplace-email-and-meeting-guide.astro

- src/pages/ai-for-parents-school-communication.astro

- src/pages/prompt-templates.astro

- src/components/PromptBox.astro

Page content goals:
Cover practical uses such as:

- writing a polite request

- responding to a frustrating message calmly

- making a message shorter and clearer

- changing tone to sound more professional or more friendly

- writing school, work, or service related emails

- turning rough notes into a clean message

- reviewing an email before sending

Important content requirements:

- explain that AI can help draft and revise, but the user should review for tone, facts, and context

- remind users not to paste sensitive account details, confidential work information, or private student records

- include copy and paste prompts for everyday email situations

- explain how to keep the final message sounding human and personal

- keep the advice relevant to normal adults, not just office workers

Suggested page structure:

- title and intro

- how AI can help with everyday emails

- how AI can help with tone and clarity

- sample prompts

- what to watch out for

- where to go next

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- direct

- calm

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not suggest AI should send messages without review

- do not turn the page into a business only page

- do not overload the page with too many prompts

- do not encourage sharing sensitive details

- do not make the page sound like a productivity app ad

Implementation requirements:

- keep the page visually consistent with the site

- use existing components where helpful

- ensure internal links work correctly

- preserve Astro structure and shared patterns

Output requirement:
Generate the full contents of the new page and any supporting updates if needed.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Write the better emails page.
