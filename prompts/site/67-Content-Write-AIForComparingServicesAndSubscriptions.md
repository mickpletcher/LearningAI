# Prompt 67

You are working inside an Astro website project called Learn AI the American Way.

Your task:
Create a practical page showing how beginners can use AI to compare services, subscriptions, plans, and recurring options more clearly.

Goal:
Help regular people use AI to think through service choices without treating AI like a final authority on pricing, contracts, or terms.

Create this new file:

- src/pages/ai-for-comparing-services-and-subscriptions.astro

Also review and update if needed:

- src/pages/ai-for-shopping-and-product-comparison.astro

- src/pages/everyday-uses.astro

- src/pages/use-cases.astro

- src/pages/prompt-templates.astro

- src/components/PromptBox.astro

Page content goals:
Cover practical uses such as:

- comparing streaming plans

- comparing cell phone or internet plans

- comparing gym memberships

- comparing software subscriptions

- comparing home services

- listing pros and cons

- turning marketing language into plain English

- building a checklist of what to compare before buying

Important content requirements:

- explain that AI can help organize comparisons and explain terms, but users should verify pricing, renewal rules, contract terms, cancellation policies, and service coverage

- remind users not to paste account numbers, payment details, or full contracts with sensitive personal data

- include copy and paste prompts for comparing services

- keep examples realistic for normal households and small business users

- make the advice practical and grounded

Suggested page structure:

- title and intro

- how AI can help compare services

- how AI can simplify confusing terms

- sample prompts

- what to watch out for

- where to go next

Tone requirements:

- plainspoken

- practical

- direct

- calm

- beginner friendly

- not robotic

- not corporate

- no hype

Important constraints:

- do not provide legal advice

- do not imply AI can detect hidden contract issues perfectly

- do not encourage sharing sensitive billing information

- do not turn the page into affiliate style content

- do not overload the page with too many prompts

Implementation requirements:

- keep the page visually consistent with the site

- use existing components where helpful

- ensure related links work correctly

- preserve Astro structure and shared patterns

Output requirement:
Generate the full contents of the new page and any supporting updates if needed.
Do not summarize.
Do not explain.
Write the services and subscriptions comparison page.
