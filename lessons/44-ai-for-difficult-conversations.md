# Lesson: AI for Difficult Conversations

## Introduction and context

Difficult conversations show up in work and personal settings.

Good preparation helps you stay clear and respectful.

AI can help structure your points before the conversation starts.

## Why AI is useful for this topic

AI can help you:
- frame facts without blame
- prepare calm opening lines
- define desired outcomes
- draft responses for possible objections

Example prompt:

Help me prepare talking points for a respectful conversation about repeated missed deadlines.

## How to ask for better help on this topic

Include:
1. who the conversation is with
2. what issue needs to be addressed
3. what outcome you want
4. the tone you want to maintain

Weak prompt:
Help me confront this person.

Better prompt:
Help me prepare a calm and direct conversation with a colleague about recurring missed deadlines. Include opening line, examples, and next steps.

## Practical examples

1. Discussing repeated lateness on deliverables.
2. Resetting expectations after communication gaps.
3. Addressing boundary issues respectfully.
4. Clarifying roles after ongoing confusion.

## Try this yourself

1. Describe one difficult conversation you are avoiding.
2. Ask AI for a respectful opening line.
3. Ask AI for three next step options to close the conversation.

## What to watch out for

1. Over scripting every word.
2. Avoiding direct language about the issue.
3. Choosing poor timing for the conversation.

## Check your understanding

### Question 1
What four details improve prompts for difficult conversations?

Answer: Person, issue, desired outcome, and tone.

### Question 2
Why should you avoid blame language?

Answer: Blame triggers defensiveness and reduces problem solving.

### Question 3
What is one practical AI use before a difficult conversation?

Answer: Drafting a calm opening line and clear next step options.
