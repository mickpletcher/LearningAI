# Lesson: AI for Professional Communication

## Introduction and context

Professional communication includes updates, requests, recaps, and decisions.

The challenge is being clear without being overly long.

AI helps structure information so people can act faster.

## Why AI is useful for this topic

AI can help you:
- organize updates by priority
- clarify requests and owners
- adjust message tone for audience level
- summarize complex details into clear language

Example prompt:

Rewrite this project update for senior leadership in under 120 words with progress, risk, and next step.

## How to ask for better help on this topic

Include:
1. audience
2. purpose
3. desired outcome
4. tone and length constraints

Weak prompt:
Improve this message.

Better prompt:
Rewrite this update for executives. Keep it concise and include decision needed by Friday.

## Practical examples

1. Weekly project status update.
2. Cross team dependency request.
3. Decision request with two options.
4. Meeting recap with owners and due dates.

## Try this yourself

1. Ask AI to rewrite one current work message.
2. Ask AI for a shorter version and a detailed version.
3. Ask AI to add one clear call to action.

## What to watch out for

1. Vague asks.
2. Missing owner or due date.
3. Over formal language that hides the point.

## Check your understanding

### Question 1
What four prompt elements improve professional communication drafts?

Answer: Audience, purpose, desired outcome, and tone or length.

### Question 2
Why should messages include explicit actions?

Answer: Clear actions reduce delays and confusion.

### Question 3
What is one risk of using AI drafts without edits?

Answer: The message can sound generic and miss team context.
